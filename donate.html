Why Your Support Matters

Your donations are critical to our mission of advancing rocketry education and innovation at UC Irvine. As a student-led project, we rely on the generosity of individuals and organizations to fund our research, development, and competition activities.

Equipment & Materials

Fund cutting-edge equipment, sensors, and materials needed for building and testing our rockets.

Competition Expenses

Help cover registration fees, travel costs, and logistics for national and international rocketry competitions.

Research & Innovation

Support our ongoing research into propulsion systems, materials science, and aerospace engineering.

Online Donation

The quickest and easiest way to support our project is through our online donation platform. All donations are processed securely through UCI's official crowdfunding platform.

In-Kind Donations

We also welcome in-kind donations of materials, equipment, software, and services that can help advance our projects. These contributions are especially valuable as they provide direct resources for our students to work with.

  • Manufacturing equipment and tools
  • Aerospace-grade materials
  • Electronic components and sensors
  • Engineering software licenses
  • Professional services (machining, testing, etc.)

Donor Recognition

We value and recognize the contributions of our donors. Depending on the level of support, donors may receive:

Acknowledgment

Recognition on our website and in project presentations

Branding

Logo placement on rockets, equipment, and team apparel

Updates

Regular updates on project progress and achievements

Events

Invitations to launches, demonstrations, and special events

For major donors, additional recognition opportunities are available. Contact us to discuss customized recognition packages.
